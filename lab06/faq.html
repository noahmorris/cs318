What is the On-Time Guarantee?

The On-Time Guarantee is our promise that we’ll show up on time for installations and service appointments, because we understand nothing is more frustrating than waiting around for an appointment. Contact us for the details of your area’s On-Time Guarantee policy.

What is identity theft?

Identity theft occurs when someone uses your personally identifiable information, such as your name or Social Security number (SSN), to access services without your authorization.
